Games & Simulation


"We all love games, why not at work"


Background

Everyone loves games, puzzles and hands-on experience. This has been one of the best ways over centuries to create lasting learning experiences.

Games and gamification approaches in the modern era brings in innovative ways to create right engagement. Be it developing a full fledge learning game or using gaming concepts to assess the learning, the gamification approach creates immersive environments for the learners.

Simulations with hands-on training and decision-branching options enhance learners’ capability to take right decisions. This approach helps employees to take better decisions at work, thereby helping the organisation grow productive and stronger. More number of efficient decision makers any organisations creates, more the organisation grows.

Our team brings in experience of implementing these solutions for several customers and we shall create lasting learning experience for your learners.


Creating right engagement

We use the combination of the following while developing game based courses:

  • Interactivities and challenges
  • Avatars
  • Branching
  • Scores
  • Virtual currencies
  • Virtual rewards / Gifts
  • Timer
  • Rewards
  • Varied levels
  • Leader boards
  • Dynamic feedback

The training should create relevant competition among employees that helps them to retain the learning for better application and performance.


Our expertise

Our team brings in experience of implementing these solutions for customers. We can work with your strategy or come up with suitable strategy from our end for the project implementation.
