Instructor-led training (ILT)

Are your ILT/VILTs helping your employees to perform better?


It is the supreme art of the teacher to awaken joy in creative expression and knowledge.

- Albert Einstein

Most training is not memorable, because it is not backed by preparation and rigor. Our instructor-led training materials for both face-to-face and virtual medium equip your instructors to provide more impactful training.

  • Do you find your employees disengaged during the training sessions?
  • Do you want your training sessions to have more impact on the performance of your employees?
  • Do you want your ILT decks and VILT sessions to look and feel great?

If your answer to any of these questions is "yes" then you are yet to benefit fully from well-designed ILT and VILT materials.

Research points out that 90% of the classroom training today aims to improve productivity and performance, whereas 69% is helping towards converting knowledge into business Outcomes and 67% is geared towards Education and Growth of Employees.


Compared to the standard ILT materials, well-designed ILTs can deliver more value for your money in the following ways:

Training that leads to better performance

We can transform content in more ways than one and create the right learning product that helps your employees assimilate the content better. As a result, your employees can perform better.

Engaging and immersive activities that bring out the best in the learners

Most training--whether face-to-face or Virtual--is designed as lectures. Thus, the learners often feel bored and do not get full value of the training session. An instructionally sound training material, with correct blending of activities and concepts, goes a long way in making the right impact on the learners.

Training decks that are designed with rich visuals

Learners can pay more attention to the instructor when the training material has the right visuals apart from text. Our team of visual designers enhance the ILT decks with visuals and designs that will create better impact on your audience.

Subject matter experts may not have the skills to design great ILT materials. Often, trainers rely on the power of their personality and words to impart training. Given this fact, you may be hard-pressed for resources to create the right and most effective training materials.

The audience will pay better attention to the ILTs when the decks are professionally designed. This is where our experts design your training materials with creative methods of instructional and visual design. Our focus is to equip your trainers improve their efficiency and effectiveness in delivering the right learning experience.

Based on the inputs received from you, our team of expert instructional designers and visual artists either enhance the existing materials or build them from scratch. Our professional team sticks to the timeline in delivering the training materials.

Considering aforesaid aspects, we can summarise our solutions as follows:

Better application of learning leading to improved performance

Your learners benefit from our effectively designed training materials as they can apply their learning to real-world situations. This leads to improved performance.

Greater return on investment (ROI) of your precious time and money

Trainers are generally subject matter experts and busy people. What is the point in engaging their time and yet not able to see the impact on the ground level? Your experts benefit greatly from our training materials that will help you achieve your training goals. The experts deliver better quality training and the audience assimilate the content better.

Right impact on your employees/ learners

A well-designed training material will help your employees participate more during the classroom sessions, assimilate the content better, and perform better after the training is done.
