Custom eLearning

Are your elearning customized to promote optimal performance?


"The most important principle for designing lively eLearning is to see eLearning design not as information design but as designing an experience"

Custom eLearning is the current trend in corporate training. Though pre-built eLearning courses are cheaper compared to custom eLearning, many corporates—both small and big—are opting for custom eLearning because they don’t want to limit their training expectations and learner engagement to pre-built, off the shelf eLearning courses.

  • Do you want to align your training content with your company’s branding and vision that improves employees’ engagement and performance?
  • Are you looking for long-term cost reduction of your training material in terms of reduced license fee and maintenance cost?
  • Are you interested in doing quick and convenient updates of your training courses to suit your changing business goals?

If your answer to any of these questions is “yes” then you need to explore custom or bespoke eLearning options than choosing pre-built training material.

The revenue for self-paced, customized online learning solutions market worldwide is expected to reach $55.2 billion by 2017. This estimate is based on a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 7.6% for the period 2011-17, starting with $35.6 billion in 2011.

The major chunk of this growth is coming from a rapid increase in use of eLearning for corporate training. Due to the high degree of flexibility of customized eLearning training material, the corporates can easily update their training content in cost efficient ways. A study conducted by IOMA finds that switching from instructor-led training (ILT) to eLearning saves corporates around 50-70% in costs.

Compared to pre-built eLearning, custom eLearning can deliver more value for your money in the following ways:

Training aligned to your company’s vision, branding, and goals

Pre-built eLearning courses are generic and therefore, are unlikely to be aligned with your company’s branding and business goals. Custom eLearning gives you full control to align training courses accurately with your company’s culture, message, and branding.

Long-term cost reduction of your training material

Custom eLearning usually costs higher initially than licensing pre-built eLearning courses but it reduces training cost in long-term, because you don’t have to pay license and maintenance fees. You save money by reusing media components in your future projects.

Quick and convenient updates of your training courses

When your training needs change depending on your revised business goals, custom eLearning allows you to do quick updates of training material because you own the source files and have been fully involved in the original course development process.
