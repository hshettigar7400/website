"Instructor led training will continue to be important"


Right engagement with learners

The classroom training will continue to be one of the core training methods across organisations. We understand the challenges of creating better engagement for the leaners in classroom setting. It is even more challenging when it is virtual classroom training.

Our unique training design strategy ensures the learners are motivated during pre-training and engaged fully during the training. We also implement good remediation mechanisms through online assessments to plug performance gaps, if any.


Development methodology

To implement the aforesaid right engagement with learners, we employ various methodologies in our training programmes.

  • Interactive sessions
  • Micro teaching
  • Roleplays
  • Group discussion and activities
  • Hands-on / skill practice discussions and activities
  • Case Studies
  • Gamification based activities (online)
  • Online Assessments

Our expertise

Our Instructional Design and Content team has hands-on experience of having crafted highly impactful classroom and virtual training material for customers across multiple domains.

They work closely with customers to understand the requirements in detail and then collate all the required information at the pre-planning phase.
