The best way to implement this is to have a strategy which will provide seamless learning experience irrespective of the device on which it is accessed. Hence, we carefully choose the strategy by working closely with the customer in creating the solution.

Our responsive framework delivery ensures the learner gets similar learning experience on all the devices (PC, Tablet and Smartphone) across the Operating Systems (iOS, Android and Windows) using our custom HTML5 framework (for responsive eLearning).
