Localisation


"Right translation and localisation is essential for successful training deployment"


We bring in vast experience and expertise in localisation and internationalisation of any material to any language. Let the requirement be localising eLearning course, voice-overs, videos or any other documentation, we will have the required solutions.

We provide the following services for localisation of eLearning courses:

  • Translation
  • Voice-over
  • Integration services

We can translate eLearning courses built using all the authoring tools such as Adobe Flash, Custom HTML5, Lectora, Articulate Storyline, Articulate Presenter and Captivate.


Team

We use native translators, experienced proof readers/linguistic experts and native voice-over artists to ensure the learners get the best localised material for learning/reading.


Languages we can handle

We can provide services in more than 30 global languages across Europe, Asia including Middle East and Latin America.
