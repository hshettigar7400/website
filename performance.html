At the moment of need

The challenge today is to provide eLearning solutions on demand and at the moment of need. This ensures compliance from the learners to apply the learning more effectively on time and to bring in better performance.

Organisations should have this as a part of their overall training delivery to have right remediation measures, which in turn will create a better learning environment and brining better collaboration among the employees.

These performance support tools can be deployed using the following forms:
